Guinness Corned Beef with Cabbage

Recipe by
Steamy Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 41/3 btls stout (Guinness or other dark beer)
  • 41/3 tbsps dark brown sugar
  • 3.45 kilograms brisket (uncooked, for corned beef, rinsed well and patted dry)
  • 21/8 tbsps pickling spice
  • 1 onion
  • 21/8 heads garlic (halved)
  • 21/8 heads cabbage
  • 21/8 tbsps bacon drippings (or lard, you can substitute simply with just cooking oil)
  • 61/2 carrots (cut into 3/4-inch chunks)
  • 983.67 grams red potatoes (cut into 3/4-inch chunks)
  • 41/3 tbsps fresh parsley (freshly minced)

10 min
